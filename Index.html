Ahenobarbus Henocied, quick history.

Ahenobarbus Henocied was an image first uploaded on a Facebook gore account in the 2000s. It was popular because it looked disturbing and cool as fuck. The original image is a Japanese doll. This doll was from a trend where you turn this doll cute again, but someone made it scary instead. The face is just put onto the doll’s head with some partial blood editing. People then referred to it as “ahenobarbus henocied” or “DollThing”. Aheno was later used in games like Nico’s Nextbots on Roblox and in nextbot mods on Gmod.

Aheno Questions

Whats the source of the face?

The facial features were traced to a 2005 music video for the song "針夢廃墟" (Shinmu Haikyo) by Ai Shimizu. The blood on the mouth was fake blood used in behind the scene shots from the music video, which were later photoshopped onto the doll’s face.

What does “Ahenobarbus henocied” mean?

We don’t really know but this is what people have decoded from it. “Ahenobarbus” is a Latin family name meaning “bronze-beard” or “red-beard.” The most famous guy from the family Nero (born Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus.) Nero was known for being cruel and persecuting Christians during the Roman Ages. “Henocied” is probably just a typo of “genocide.” Which partially makes sense.

Is it a real person?

No. Aheno isn’t real. Its a doll with an edited face. And “Ahenobarbus Henocied” isn’t an individual. At least not thats been recorded and documented.
